Sandwich Recipe

To make a delicious vegetable sandwich, start by taking two slices of bread and spreading butter or mayonnaise on them. You can also add green chutney or tomato ketchup for extra flavor. Then, place thin slices of vegetables like tomato, cucumber, boiled potato, and onion on one slice of bread. Optionally, you can add a cheese slice or some grated cheese. Sprinkle a little salt, pepper, and chaat masala to enhance the taste. Cover it with the second slice of bread. You can eat the sandwich as it is, or toast it on a pan with some butter until both sides turn golden brown. Once ready, cut it in half and serve it with chutney or ketchup. It's a quick, easy, and tasty snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, brown, or multigrain)
  • 1 small tomato (thinly sliced)
  • 1 small cucumber (thinly sliced)
  • 1 small onion (optional, thinly sliced)
  • 1 boiled potato (optional, sliced)
  • 1 slice of cheese or grated cheese (optional)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Green chutney or tomato ketchup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Take 2 slices of fresh bread
  2. Spread butter or mayonnaise
  3. Add a layer of green chutney or tomato ketchup for extra taste.
  4. Place thin slices of vegetables like cucumber, tomato, boiled potato, and onion on one slice of bread.
  5. Add a cheese slice or grated cheese if you like.
  6. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper, and optionally some chaat masala for flavor
  7. Cover with the second slice of bread to complete the sandwich.
  8. Toast or grill the sandwich on a pan with butter until it turns golden and crispy.
  9. Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters.
